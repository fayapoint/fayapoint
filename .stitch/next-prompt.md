A jaw-dropping, futuristic landing page for an AI education and automation platform targeting Brazilian entrepreneurs. Premium SaaS aesthetic inspired by Stripe, Linear, and Vercel — dark theme with glassmorphism effects, gradient accents, and generous whitespace. All text in Portuguese (Brazilian).

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first (fully responsive)
- Theme: Dark, futuristic, premium, cinematic depth
- Background: Deep Charcoal (#030712) with subtle radial gradient overlays
- Surface: Frosted Glass (#ffffff08) with backdrop-blur for card elevation
- Primary Accent: Electric Purple (#8b5cf6) for primary CTAs and highlights
- Secondary Accent: Hot Pink (#ec4899) for secondary actions and gradients
- Tertiary Accent: Neon Cyan (#06b6d4) for data visualizations and badges
- Gradient Hero: Linear gradient sweep from Purple (#8b5cf6) through Pink (#ec4899) to Cyan (#06b6d4)
- Text Primary: Near White (#f8fafc) for headings
- Text Secondary: Soft Gray (#94a3b8) for body text and descriptions
- Text Gradient: Gradient text effect on hero headline (purple → pink → cyan)
- Border: Subtle glass borders (#ffffff10) on all cards
- Buttons Primary: Gradient fill (purple → pink), rounded (10px), subtle glow shadow
- Buttons Secondary: Transparent with glass border, white text, hover glow
- Cards: Rounded (16px), glassmorphism (bg-white/5, backdrop-blur-xl, border-white/10), hover: border brightens
- Shadows: Colored glow shadows matching accent colors (0 0 40px rgba(139,92,246,0.15))
- Typography: Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans, bold headings (tracking-tight), clean body text
- Spacing: Generous — 80px+ section padding, 32px card gaps
- Animations: Subtle float on hero elements, fade-up on scroll reveal
