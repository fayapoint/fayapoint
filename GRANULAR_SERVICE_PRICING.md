Ranges follow 2024–2025 industry benchmarks gathered from HeyOrca, HawkSEM, Cloud Campaign, Hypetrix, SlateRock, Designow, FireArt, FreshBooks, Covalent, RK Color, Upwork, BetterBuys, and related research.

> **Unit key**
> - `min` / `max`: observed market range
> - `rec`: recommended anchor price (default quote)
> - `unit`: billing basis (per page, minute, workflow, etc.)
